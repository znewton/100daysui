About

This is a site for me to work on strengthening my front-end development skills

There is a challenge online in the world of Designers called the 100 Days UI challenge, where a designer will receive prompts (typically through email) and design a UI around that. This continues each day for 100 days.

My goal for this site is to take a designer's resulting daily UI's and turn them into a static web page using CSS3, Vanilla JS, and HTML5. For my inspirational designer, I will be using Paul Flavius, the creator of 100daysui.com.
